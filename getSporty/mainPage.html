General news


Atletico Madrid have confirmed goalkeeper Jan Oblak has undergone scans for a groin injury, with an injury layoff likely. The Slovenian missed the first leg of the Europa League clash with Lokomotiv Moscow on Thursday and Marca previously reported he will also sit out the weekend clash with Celta Vigo.

Mohamed Salah has picked up the Premier League's Player of the Month award for a second time this season after maintaining his sensational scoring form in February. The Egypt international - who also claimed the honour in November - found the net four times in three fixtures last month, as well as providing two assists.
